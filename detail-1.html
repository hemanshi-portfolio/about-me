Workspace Design - 1

Design Brief

  • Objective: To create a modern, functional, and stylish office that promotes productivity, teamwork, and comfort.
  • Office Design: Modern interior with sleek wooden accent walls, neutral tones, and elegant textures.

    • Specific Areas

  • Private Office: Textured wooden wall, minimalist desk, and shelving.
  • Lounge Area: Cozy sofa, patterned armchairs, and coffee table.
  • Collaborative Workspace: Multiple workstations and ergonomic chairs.
  • Reception Area: Green sofa, stylish coffee table, and decorative partition.
  • Waiting Area: Comfortable seating, patterned rug, and accent wall.
  • Conclusion: Blending style, comfort, and functionality in office design.
