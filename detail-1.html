Workspace Design - 1

Design Brief

  • Objective : To create a modern, functional, and stylish office that promotes productivity, teamwork, and comfort. Each space has a unique purpose, ensuring a cohesive yet distinct look throughout the office.
  • Office Design : Modern interior with sleek wooden accent walls, neutral tones, and elegant textures for a professional atmosphere.

    • Specific Areas

  • Private Office : Textured wooden wall, minimalist desk, and elegant shelving for a refined and focused workspace.
  • Lounge Area : Cozy sofa, patterned armchairs, and sleek coffee table for informal meetings and relaxation.
  • Collaborative Workspace : Multiple workstations, ergonomic chairs, and ample storage to boost productivity and teamwork.
  • Reception Area : Modern design with a green sofa, stylish coffee table, and decorative partition for a welcoming entrance.
  • Waiting Area : Comfortable seating, patterned rug, and accent wall to create a warm and inviting space for visitors.
  • Conclusion : This brief aims to create an office that blends style, comfort, and functionality, ensuring each area serves its purpose while enhancing the overall office appeal.
