Workspace Design - 2 (Advocate Office)

  • To create a modern, elegant, and welcoming salon environment that enhances the client experience.
  • Each area is designed with a distinct purpose while maintaining a cohesive aesthetic throughout the salon.
  • Features a sleek reception desk, modern lighting, and a comfortable waiting area with a blue sectional sofa. To provide a warm and inviting entrance for clients
  • Includes a comfortable seating arrangement with a blue sofa, accented with soft lighting and stylish decor.
  • To offer a relaxing space for clients to wait before their appointments.
  • Features built-in shelving with arch designs to showcase products, paired with consultation chairs. To facilitate product viewing and consultations.
  • Equipped with modern salon chairs, washing stations, and ambient lighting. To provide a comfortable and functional space for various salon treatments. A well-lit area with multiple styling stations, mirrors, and greenery accents.
  • To create an efficient and aesthetically pleasing space for hair styling and makeup application. An elegant private lounge with bold floral wallpaper, a stylish chair, and a full-length mirror
  • This area is for bridal photoshoot. Features comfortable seating and dedicated nail treatment stations with adequate lighting and storage.
  • To provide a specialized area for manicures and pedicures. The salon entrance with a clean and modern design, featuring the salon’s logo and subtle lighting. To create a strong first impression and attract clients.
  • Soft pastel tones combined with pops of blue and green to create a calming yet sophisticated atmosphere.
  • Conclusion : This design brief aims to transform the office space into a harmonious blend of style, comfort, and functionality, ensuring that each area serves its intended purpose while contributing to the overall aesthetic appeal of the office.
